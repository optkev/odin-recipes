Pasta Salad

Amazing pasta with tomatoes,pepperoni, and provolone. This is great as a easy meal to make, and good for side dishes.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz fusilli pasta
  • 3 cups tomatoes
  • 1/2 lb salami
  • 1/4 lb pepperoni
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 can of black olives
  • 1 (4 oz) jar pimentos
  • 1 bottle italian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Get a large pot of water boiling, put the fusilli into it, stirring occasionally, cook for around 12 minutes and then drain
  2. In a bowl mix the fusilli, tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green pepper, olives, and pimentos. Add salad dressing and mix
