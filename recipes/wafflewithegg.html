Waffle egg in a hole..Made with Air Fryer!

When your in need of something to eat fast, that has a little sugar and protein, look no further!

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen waffle
  • 1 egg
  • salt and pepper
  • shredded cheese
  • maple syrup

Steps

  1. Set air fryer to 350 degrees.
  2. Cut hole in waffle, add a layer of parchment paper to bottom of the air fryer and set waffle on top.
  3. Crack egg into the hole of waffle; season. set cook time to 5-6 minutes.
  4. When done, add cheese to top of waffle and cook until cheese melts.
  5. Take waffle out and transfer to plate, add syrup and enjoy.
