Grilled cheese with apple and bacon

Everything is better with bacon, right? If so then this is the meal for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your choice of bread
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of apple
  • 2 bacon strips (cooked)

Steps

  1. lay out ingredients and put together your sandwich
  2. Get pan with butter in it ready
  3. Put sandwich in pan and cook on high until cheese has melt and bread is golden brown
